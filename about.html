Our history

We are Sebratec AB.

Everything started with 2 former consultants getting to know each other whilst working for Volvo Cars in Torslanda, Gothenburg. After that, they soon realised to share the same ambition of doing something bigger not only to the tech industry but also for people's lives. This is the exact moment that Sebratec AB first get into the picture. The company would be an environment for tech enthusiats, a perfect environment to gather professionals of excellence that share the same strong belief in the future. Both Sebratec's founders, Denni and Renan, have strong roots in Brazil. Therefore, it didn't take too long to realise that Sebratec was also the perfect opportunity to bring both countries together.

A Sweden based company providing opportunties to Brazilian professionals.

Since then, Sebratec has been the bridge between high qualified professionals from Brazil and the technology industry in Sweden. In just 4 years, Sebratec has come from a this common dream of 2 individuals to a team of more than 30 professionals, having the head office in the Gothenburg area but also a branch in São Paulo, Brazil. Since its creation, the company has expanded its expertise in a way that nowadays is being able to provide high end solutions when it comes to technology and consulting services.
